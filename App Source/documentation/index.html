Support Directory

You required two supporting directory

1. google-play-service_lib

2. appcompat_v7

 

for import two diectory go to following

Magic Tips > Properties > Android

If not in Library list. Click on " Add " button and import library from followinf location.

Google Play Lib :

android-sdk-windows\extras\google\google_play_services\libproject\google-play-services_lib

 

App Compat V7 :

android-sdk-windows\extras\android\support\v7

  1. Since most of the questions are asked over and over again a searchable forum will make it possible for buyer to get quick answer of their problem without need to wait for our response.
